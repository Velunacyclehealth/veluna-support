Privacy Policy for Veluna Cycle Health

Last updated: April 2026

Introduction

Veluna Cycle Health is a cycle and period tracking wellness app designed to help users log cycle information, journal privately, and access gentle self-care and guidance tools.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Veluna Cycle Health may store, how that information is used, and the choices available to you. Veluna Cycle Health is a wellness companion and is not a medical diagnosis or treatment service.

Information We Collect

Veluna Cycle Health may store information that you choose to enter into the app, including:

  • Period dates and cycle settings
  • Journal entries written in free-form text fields
  • App preferences and selected themes
  • Saved quotes, streak data, and related in-app wellness information

The app does not collect location data, contacts, photos, camera input, microphone input, or files from your device.

The app does not use HealthKit, does not include third-party login, and does not require you to create an account.

How We Use Information

Information stored in Veluna Cycle Health is used to provide app features and improve your experience, including:

  • Showing your logged cycle and period information
  • Saving journal entries and personal preferences
  • Supporting premium features and subscription access through Apple StoreKit
  • Providing local gratitude reminder notifications if you allow notifications

iCloud / Apple Sync

Data in Veluna Cycle Health is primarily stored on your device. If you are signed into your Apple account and iCloud is available, some app data may also sync through Apple’s iCloud services to help keep your information available across your own Apple devices.

Data Sharing

Veluna Cycle Health does not sell your personal data. Veluna Cycle Health does not share your personal data with advertisers, data brokers, or third-party analytics providers.

The app does not use third-party advertising SDKs and does not track you across apps or websites.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your app data remains stored on your device and, when available, may also be stored in your iCloud account for sync purposes.

You can remove your information by deleting entries inside the app or by deleting the app from your device. If data has synced through iCloud, you may also need to remove related iCloud data from your Apple account settings.

Children’s Privacy

Veluna Cycle Health is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can help address the issue.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
