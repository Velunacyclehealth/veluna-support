Privacy Policy for Veluna Cycle Health

Veluna Cycle Health stores information you enter into the app, which may include cycle tracking data, journal entries, app preferences, and related wellness information.

If iCloud sync is enabled, some app data may be synced through your iCloud account to keep your information available across your devices.

Veluna Cycle Health does not sell your personal data.
