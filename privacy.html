Privacy Policy for Veluna Cycle Health

Veluna Cycle Health stores information you enter into the app, which may include cycle tracking data, journal entries, app preferences, and related wellness information.

If you are signed into iCloud and iCloud is available for the app, some app data may sync through your iCloud account to help keep your information available across your devices.

Veluna Cycle Health does not sell your personal data. Veluna Cycle Health does not share your personal data with advertisers, data brokers, or third-party analytics providers.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is stored on your device and, when available, may also be stored in your iCloud account for syncing purposes. You can remove data by deleting entries in the app or by deleting the app and any related iCloud data from your devices and account settings.

Contact
